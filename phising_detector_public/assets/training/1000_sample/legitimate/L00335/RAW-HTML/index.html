      Quiz: How many drugs has the FDA approved for Alzheimer's?

      You are correct!

      Incorrect

      The correct answer is: Testicular

      Of the 244 potential Alzheimer's drugs tested in people over 10 years, only one of them earned FDA approval. The lone approval was for memantine (Namenda), used to treat dementia linked to Alzheimer’s disease.
