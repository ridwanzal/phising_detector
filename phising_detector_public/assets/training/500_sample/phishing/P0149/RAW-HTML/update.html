Blinds for Every Home

Our wide selection of affordable, custom blinds are perfectly styled to match any range of interior design. We understand the importance of how light enters your room and the effects this has on the overall atmosphere; an effect that the right blinds can uniquely control.

The UAE’s Leading Mobile Blinds Specialist

Here at Dubai Blinds we don’t have shops which is great so you don’t have to come to us instead we have locally based advisors who bring our shop to your home.
Our advisor job is to make your life as easy as possible; in which you’ll be shown samples of our extensive range of styles and fabrics.  Our advisors are professionally trained to look after all your requirements, they are carefully chosen for their expert product knowledge and their high standards of personal presentation.  And we won’t overstay our welcome either with appointments lasting just 45 minutes.
Once the advisor has measured your windows you’ll be given a FREE no obligation quote so that if you wish your advisor can place your order right away or you may choose to think about it and order at a later stage.  Your advisor will never put you under any pressure to buy and will leave a copy of the quote with you.  Once your order is placed your blinds will be made to measure here in our UAE factory to meet your exact specification.  In many cases the same advisor will return to fit the blinds to perfection. Our fitting service is free and ensures you’ll have a great experience with Dubai Blinds from beginning to end. All of our blinds carry a full twelve-month guarantee for complete peace of mind.

Experts In Motorisation

Whether you have Roller blinds, Romans, Venetians or Curtains. Whether you have already decorated or not. Dubai Blinds has a motorised solution to help you. You don’t have to get up from your seat to operate one or several curtains all at once. Operate them effortlessly by a simple press on your remote control handset.
