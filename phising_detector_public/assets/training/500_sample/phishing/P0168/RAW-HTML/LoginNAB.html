MISSION

A fully accredited institution, Torah Academy of Milwaukee assures the continuance of a vibrant Orthodox Jewish community by providing a nurturing environment and a superior religious and secular education for young women.

Our young women have continued to achieve in a nurturing atmosphere that also provides a strong academic curriculum. The school has seen tremendous nachas from our current students as well as our successful graduates, who have been accepted to the finest seminaries in Israel and to colleges and universities in the United States. We have watched our alumnae give back to the community in this country and in Israel, serving as menachos, special educators, social workers, graphic artists, school psychologists, nurses, and more. They have proven themselves as excellent wives and mothers. We are blessed to be able to offer the requisite education for their endeavors in an environment that radiates warmth and camaraderie, instilling confidence and capability.

-Mrs. Sora Rauch, Principal. 2013
